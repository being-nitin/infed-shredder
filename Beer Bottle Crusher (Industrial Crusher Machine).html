Beer Bottle Crusher (Industrial Crusher Machine)

Product Overview:

A beer bottle crusher machine is designed to efficiently crush and recycle glass bottles, specifically beer bottles. This equipment helps in managing waste by reducing the volume of glass bottles, making them easier to handle and process. It's commonly used in bars, restaurants, breweries, and recycling facilities.

Specification:

  • Motor Power: Ranges from 1 kW to 5 kW depending on the model and capacity.
  • Crushing Capacity: Can handle up to 1000 kg/hr of glass bottles.
  • Dimensions: Typically around 1000 mm x 800 mm x 1500 mm.
  • Weight: Approximately 300 kg.
