Conveyor

Product Overview:

A screw conveyor, also known as an auger conveyor, is a mechanical device used to transport bulk materials in various industries. It utilizes a rotating helical screw blade (or auger) to move materials along a trough or tube, making it ideal for handling powders, granules, and small particles.

Specification:

  • Screw Diameter: Typically ranges from 100 mm to 1000 mm, depending on the model and application.
  • Conveyor Length: Can be customized to various lengths, generally from 1 meter to over 10 meters.
  • Capacity: Varies based on screw diameter and speed, typically ranging from 0.1 to 100 cubic meters per hour.
  • Material Construction: Often made from stainless steel, carbon steel, or other durable materials based on the application.
