Dewatering Machine

Dewatering Machine:

A dewatering machine is designed to remove water from various types of solid materials, including industrial sludge, food waste, agricultural by-products, and other materials that require reduction in water content. These machines are essential in industries where waste management and material processing are critical.

Specification:

  • Motor Power: Ranges from 20 kW to 100 kW depending on the model and capacity.
  • Processing Capacity: Can handle up to 5000 kg/hr of various waste materials.
  • Dimensions: Vary by model, generally around 4000 mm x 2500 mm x 3000 mm.
  • Weight: Approximately 3000 kg.
